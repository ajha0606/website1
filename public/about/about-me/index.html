About Me

December 15, 2019   

HELLO!

My name is Alexis Ha. I am currently a 4th year Pre-Dental student at the University of Texas at Austin and am planning to graduate with a BS in Human Biology in May 2020. I was born and raised in the Lone Star State (Texas, y’all!) in the Dallas metroplex, which will forever be my home. In my free time, I love spending time with family and close friends, as well as my dog! My love for dogs has led me to volunteering at a local animal shelter, which has made me fall in love with dogs even more, and having been vegan for over 4 years, I have come to have so much more compassion for animals than I ever have. I also love to work out, bake, watch movies, and binge TV shows!

LEADERSHIP Over the course of my college career, I became very involved with a church I joined in Austin. During my sophomore year, I led a ministry called Jesus Cakes, and during my junior year, I became a small group leader. Although these were some of the toughest things I had to endure, they were both very rewarding, especially because my faith and beliefs are very important to me.

SKILLS I am proficient in Microsoft Word and Powerpoint, and have a base-level knowledge of RStudio and Python. I am always willing and happy to be able to learn more and improve my skills!

ACCOMPLISHMENTS In 2018, I was named a College Scholar. College Scholars included students who have completed 60+ hours of coursework and were ranked in the top 20% of their class in their respective colleges, based on GPA.

CONTACT If you have any questions, concerns, comments, or would just like to talk, you can reach me best via email at ajha0606@gmail.com
